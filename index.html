IBIS Model Builder

Generate a board-level .ibs model in your browser — from device geometry, from measured edge rates, or from raw I‑V/V‑t data. 100% client-side.

Estimate, not sign-off. The W/L path uses long-channel square-law physics and will overstate drive at modern nodes. Trust the Rise/Fall and Paste Data paths more than the geometry path. Always correlate against SPICE or silicon before real use.

Run the above in the Virtuoso CIW. It lists every instance on the PAD net with its W/L — your drivers are the pfet (pull-up) and nfet (pull-down) rows. If nothing prints, the net name didn't match your pattern — the script then lists the cell's actual net names so you can fix the pattern. Most reliable path: it also writes a SPICE netlist you can paste into the Paste SPICE netlist tab.
